About Crimson Defense

Est. 2018

Who We Are

Crimson Defense Cyber Security Club is the premier cyber security student organization affiliated with the University of Alabama Department of Computer Science. We strive to create a community on campus for those who are interested in all things cyber security: from cyber crime to digital forensics to malware analysis and everything in between! We host General Interest meetings on Wednesday evenings, and Competition Team meetings every other Tuesday evening during the Fall and Spring semesters. Be sure to join our Discord for the latest meeting information.

Find us on mySOURCE!

University of Alabama students who are interested in getting involved with Crimson Defense Cyber Security Club should join our Discord!

Missions

  • Teach real, applicable security skills (penetration testing, malware analysis, etc.)

  • Compete in cyber security competitions at the local, state, and national level. See our Competition Team page!

  • Inspire the next generation of cyber professionals. See our Outreach page!

  • Advocate for cyber security curriculum at the University of Alabama. Our work in this area has helped to create the new College of Engineering Cyber Security Major and Computer Science Concentration in Cyber Security at UA!
