What is Crimson Crossover?

Crimson Crossover is an annual hybrid cyber security and computer science competition held between the University of Alabama's ACM chapter and Crimson Defense Cyber Security Club. Competitors compete in three rounds of CTF and competitive programming to accumulate points based on the number of puzzles they solve. There are two ways to participate: as a competitor, representing either ACM or Crimson Defense, or as an audience member. There will be separate beginner-friendly challenges for audience members to participate in as well as prizes!

Compete or Participate in Crimson Crossover:
